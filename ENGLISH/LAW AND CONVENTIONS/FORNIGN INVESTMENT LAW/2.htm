Decision on the Amendment of the Law

of the People’s Republic of China on Chinese-foreign Joint Ventures

(Adopted at the fourth session of the Ninth National People’s Congress on March 15, 2001)

The fourth session of the Ninth National People’s Congress has decided to make the following amendments to the Law of the People’s Republic of China on Chinese-foreign Joint Ventures:

I. Change the second paragraph of Article 2 into: Joint ventures shall follow the provisions of the laws and regulations of the People's Republic of China in all their activities.

II. Change the fourth paragraph of Article 6 into: Contracts shall be entered into in accordance with the law to prescribe the recruitment, dismissal, remuneration, welfare, labor protection, labor insurance, etc..

III. An article shall be added as Article 7: The staff employees of the joint venture establishes trade unions in accordance with the law, carry out the activities of the trade union and defend the lawful rights and interests of the employees.

Joint ventures shall provide necessary conditions for the activities of the trade unions thereof.

IV. Change Article 8 into Article 9, the fourth paragraph of which is changed into: All insurances of joint ventures shall be procured at the insurance companies within the territory of the People’s Republic of China.

V. Delete the first paragraph of Article 9.

VI. Change Article 9 into Article 10. The second paragraph of Article 9 in the original is changed into the first paragraph of Article 10, which goes: The Joint venture may purchase the materials such as raw materials, fuels, etc. as needed within the approved scope of business either on the domestic or international market according to the principle of fairness and reasonableness.

VII. Change Article 14 into Article 15. A paragraph shall be added as the second paragraph: Where no arbitration clauses have been included in the joint venture contract or no written arbitration agreement have been reached after a dispute arises, any party may bring a suit with the People’s court.

VIII. The provision of Article 15 that The power to amend this Law remains with the National People’s Congress is deleted.

In addition, corresponding adjustments shall be made in accordance with this decision concerning the sequence of the relevant articles.

This decision comes into force on the day of promulgation.

The Law of the People’s Republic of China on Chinese-foreign Joint Ventures shall be amended in accordance with this law and promulgated anew.

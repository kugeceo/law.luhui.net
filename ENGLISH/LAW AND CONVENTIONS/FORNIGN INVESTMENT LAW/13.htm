The Decision of the State Council on Amending the Regulations of the People¡¯s Republic of China on Sino-foreign Cooperation in the Exploitation of Continental Petroleum Resources is hereby promulgated and shall come into force on the day of promulgation.

Sep.23, 2001

Decision of the State Council on Amending the Regulations of the People¡¯s Republic of China on Sino-foreign Cooperation in the Exploitation of Continental Petroleum Resources

The State Council has decided to make the following amendments to the Regulations of the People¡¯s Republic of China on Sino-foreign Cooperation in the Exploitation of Continental Petroleum Resources:

1. The departments or units authorized by the State Council in Article 6 shall be amended as the departments designated by the State Council.

2. The China National Petroleum Corporation (CNPC) in Article 7 shall be amended as the China National Petroleum Group Company (CNPGC), China Petroleum and Chemical Group Company (hereinafter referred to the Chinese oil companies).

3. The CNPC in Article 8, Article 9 and Article 13 shall be amended as the Chinese oil companies; and the departments or units authorized by the State Council in Article 9 shall be amended as the departments designated by the State Council.

4. The second paragraph of Article 15 shall be amended as: The shares of petroleum due to foreign contractors, if to be sold within the territory of the People¡¯s Republic of China generally, shall be bought up by the Chinese oil companies, and may also be sold by other means as agreed upon by both parties to the contract, without violations of the provisions of the state on selling petroleum products within the territory of the People¡¯s Republic of China.

5. Article 16 shall be amended as: When opening foreign exchange accounts or transacting other foreign exchange matters, the foreign contractors shall abide by the Regulations of the People¡¯s Republic of China on Foreign Exchange Control and other provisions of the state on foreign exchange control.

The investment of the foreign contractors shall be US dollars or other convertible currencies.

6. Article 17 shall be amended as: Foreign contractors shall open branch companies, subsidiary companies or representative offices within the territory of the People¡¯s Republic of China according to law.

The place where the agencies mentioned in the preceding paragraph are located shall be determined by the foreign contractors and the Chinese oil companies through consultation.

7. Article 18 shall be deleted.

8. Article 19 shall be renumbered as Article 18, Article 20 shall be renumbered as Article 19 and Article 25 shall be renumbered as Article 24, and the CNPC in the three articles shall be amended as the Chinese oil companies.

9. Article 21 shall be renumbered as Article 20 and the departments or units concerned authorized by the State Council shall be amended as the departments designated by the State Council.

10. Article 22 shall be renumbered as Article 21 and amended as: The personnel needed in oil exploitation operations may be agreed upon in the oil contract, and the operators may give preference to Chinese citizens in employment.

11. Article 23, Article 26 shall be renumbered as Article 22, Article 25 respectively, the contents of which are not amended.

12. Article 27 shall be renumbered as Article 26, and the departments or units authorized by the State Council shall give a warning for correction within a prescribed time limit shall be amended as the departments designated by the State Council shall order the acts to be corrected within a prescribed time limit and give warnings; the CNPC shall be amended as the Chinese oil companies.

13. One article is added as Article 30: The China United Coalbed Methane Corporation, Ltd. shall have the exclusive right to the Sino-Chinese cooperative exploitation of coalbed methane, and these Regulations shall be referred to in the implementation.

In addition, the order of the clauses has been adjusted correspondingly.

This Decision shall come into force on the day of promulgation.

The Regulations of the People¡¯s Republic of China on Sino-foreign Cooperation in the Exploitation of Continental Petroleum Resources shall be amended correspondingly according to this Decision, and be promulgated over again.

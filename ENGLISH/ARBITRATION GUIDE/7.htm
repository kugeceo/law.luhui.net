ARBITRATION COSTS
 

¡¡¡¡¡¡When applying for arbitration, the parties shall pay certain fees in advance, which include:

¡¡¡¡¡¡I. The arbitration fee
¡¡¡¡¡¡The arbitration fee shall be paid in advance by the claimant when applying for arbitration or by the respondent when lodging his counterclaim. The collection of the arbitration fee shall be based on the Arbitration Fee Schedule, which is carried in the pamphlet of CIETAC Arbitration Rules. The arbitration tribunal shall decide in the arbitral award which party shall bear the arbitration fee or how much each party shall share.

¡¡¡¡¡¡II. The actual costs
¡¡¡¡¡¡The actual costs include those costs for engaging expert to make appraisal and expenses necessary for the arbitrators from places other than Shenzhen to attend the hearing or to make investigations and collect evidence. The arbitration tribunal shall decide which party shall pay the deposit for making investigations and collecting evidence, and those for engaging some experts to make appraisal; and the actual expenses for the arbitrator outside Shenzhen to attend the hearing shall be paid in advance by the party who appoints him/her. The arbitration tribunal shall decide in the arbitral award, which party shall bear those actual fees and expenses, or how much each party shall share.

¡¡¡¡¡¡The winning party is entitled to request the losing party to pay as compensation a proportion of the expenses reasonably incurred by it in dealing with the case.

¡¡¡¡¡¡Up to now, CIETAC has set up three Arbitration Fee Schedules. According to the difference of the nature of the disputes, the Foreign-related, Domestic and Financial Arbitration Fee Schedules will apply respectively.

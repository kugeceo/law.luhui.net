Procedure of Applying for Arbitration
 

　　　I. When applying for arbitration, the claimant must submit to Secretariat of South China Sub-Commissions the following documents:

　　　(I) The Application for Arbitration in original

The following shall be specified in the Application for Arbitration:
　　　 (1) the name and address of the claimant and those of the respondent, including the zip code, telephone number, telex number, fax number and cable number or any other means of electronic telecommunications, if any;
　　　 (2) the arbitration clause/agreement relied upon by the claimant;
　　　 (3) the claimant¨s claim;
　　　 (4) the facts and evidence on which the claimant¨s claim is based.
　　　 The Application for Arbitration shall be signed and/or stamped by the claimant and/or the attorney authorized by the claimant.

　　　(II) Documentary evidences
Documentary evidences in copy, which support the facts on which the claimant¨s claim is based, should be attached to the Application for Arbitration and to be numbered as Attachment 1,2,3´(the original one should be provided for examination while hearing)

　　　(III) The registration certificate or personal identification
　　　 The claimant¨s business license, registration certificate or personal identification should be in five copies. If the claimant is from Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan or foreign countries, this relevant documents as well as Power of Attorney should be notarized or attested.
　　　 The above-mentioned documents should be submitted in five copies (if the number of the Respondents is more than two, additional copies shall be provided accordingly), if the arbitral tribunal is composed of only one arbitrator, the number of copies submitted may be reduced by two.

　　　II. Appointing an arbitrator from among the Panel of Arbitrators of CIETAC or authorize the Chairman of CIETAC to make such appointment.

　　　III. Paying an arbitration fee in advance to South China Sub-Commission according to the Notice of Acceptance of the Case issued by the Secretariat of South China Sub-Commission.

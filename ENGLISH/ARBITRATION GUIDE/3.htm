WHY ARBITRATION IN CIETAC?
 

　　　CIETAC has become one of the major commercial arbitration centers around the world. Arbitration in CIETAC has the following advantages, especially in China:

　　 Independence and impartiality
　　　CIETAC is established by China Chamber of International Commerce (CCIC), a non-governmental institution and does not attach itself to any administrative organs and other official institutions. It independently and impartially resolves disputes between parties only according to facts and law and is not subject to any outer interference.

　　　International Arbitrators with Specialized Knowledge
　　　The present Panels of Arbitrators, in which there are more than 600 arbitrators, including one-third foreigners from more than thirty countries. Most of them are famous specialists or scholars in legal, commercial, financial and technical fields.

　　　Internationalized Arbitration Rules
　　　CIETAC revised its arbitration rules respectively in 1988, 1994, 1995, 1998 and 2000, and made its Financial Disputes Arbitration Rules effective as from May 8, 2003. Now the Arbitration Rules of CIEATC have become much more international and flexible.

　　　International and national recognition of its awards
　　　"1958 New York Convention" became effective to China on April 22, 1987, which facilitates enforcement of CIETAC's awards in all contracting states.

　　　According to Chinese law, if one party fails to comply with a CIETAC's award, the other party may apply for its enforcement to the intermediate people's court of the place where the party against whom the application for enforcement is made has his domicile or where his property is located.

　　　Up to now, hundreds of awards made by CIETAC have been recognized and enforced successfully in China and in more than 30 foreign countries.
